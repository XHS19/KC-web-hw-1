Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a first-person shooter video game
developed by Infinity Ward for the Xbox One, PlayStation 4 and Windows.
It is a reboot of the original Modern Warfare trilogy. Part of the Modern
Warfare series, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is the sixteenth game in the
Call of Duty franchise. Announced on May 30th, 2019, the game was
released on October 25th, 2019.
